About

I am finishing my master's degree in computational neuroscience, with experience modeling neural networks and analyzing complex neural datasets. Before my master's, I worked as a research assistant in a translational neuroscience lab.

My interests have evolved beyond the brain to encompass adaptivity and complex systems everywhere. I am especially interested in applying principles of decision making and learning to human collectives, and helping characterize the dynamics leading into and out of suboptimal steady states.
